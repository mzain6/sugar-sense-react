About Sugar Sense

🎯 Our Mission

Sugar Sense is dedicated to early diabetes detection using artificial intelligence. We believe that early identification of diabetes risk can help individuals take proactive steps toward better health.

🔬 How Our Model Works

Our prediction engine uses a Random Forest Classifier, a powerful machine learning algorithm that combines multiple decision trees to make accurate predictions.

Features Analyzed:

  • Pregnancies - Historical pregnancies indicate metabolic changes
  • Glucose Level - Key indicator of diabetes development
  • Blood Pressure - Associated with diabetes complications
  • Skin Thickness - Correlates with insulin resistance
  • Insulin - Shows pancreatic function
  • BMI - Body composition affects diabetes risk
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function - Genetic predisposition
  • Age - Risk increases with age

⚠️ Disclaimer

This tool is for educational and informational purposes only and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions. The predictions made by this tool are estimates based on historical data patterns and may not be 100% accurate.
